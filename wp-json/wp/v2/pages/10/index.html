You’ll be learning techniques from multiple different arts and will work on determining which techniques work most effectively for you. Every technique will be drilled over and over until it becomes effortless, and you’ll put each of them to the test in friendly sparring matches.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Forms, poomsae, tao, or kata will not be drilled unless specifically requested, however, techniques derived directly from them will be introduced, drilled, and tested in sparring.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

If you happen to have some techniques of your own that you want to practice and introduce to the class, Jasen will often be more than happy to change focus and explore those techniques with you and try out what you have to offer.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Training will be presented in an informal, friendly environment. With that said, respect and self discipline, among other things, are still a requirement. Attire, depending on the day, will be a mix of sensible everyday street clothes, workout clothes, and an option to wear a more traditional martial arts uniform upon request.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Participation in tournaments and bouts will be highly encouraged.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
